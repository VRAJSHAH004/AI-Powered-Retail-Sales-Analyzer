# AI-Powered Retail Sales Analyzer

## Project Overview

This project analyzes retail sales data using **Python** and **Google Gemini AI** to generate business insights automatically.

The application cleans retail sales data, calculates key business metrics (KPIs), creates visualizations, and generates an AI-powered executive business report that can be exported as PDF and TXT.

---

## Problem Statement

Many small businesses have raw sales data but lack the time or technical expertise to analyze it effectively.

This project automates the complete analysis process by:

- Cleaning the dataset
- Calculating business KPIs
- Creating insightful charts
- Generating an executive report using Google Gemini AI

---

- Data Cleaning
- KPI Calculation
- Business Insights

## Key Business Metrics

- Total Sales
- Total Profit
- Profit Margin
- Total Orders
- Total Quantity Sold
- Average Sales per Order
- Average Profit per Order
- Average Discount
- Business Coverage (States, Cities, Regions, Categories)

---

## Visualizations

The project includes:

- Sales by Category
- Profit by Category
- Sales by Region
- Profit by Region (Treemap)
- Sales by Segment

---

## AI Integration

Google Gemini AI automatically generates a professional business report containing:

- Executive Summary
- Key Findings
- Business Recommendations
- Areas of Concern
- Conclusion

The report is exported as:

- PDF
- TXT

---

## Project Workflow

1. Load Dataset
2. Clean Data
3. Calculate KPIs
4. Generate Charts
5. Generate Business Insights
6. Send Summary to Google Gemini AI
7. Export Report (PDF & TXT)
